About Jun Sung Lee, CPA

As a Certified Public Accountant specializing in small business taxation, I understand the unique challenges that entrepreneurs and small business owners face. My mission is to provide personalized, strategic tax solutions that help your business thrive.

My Approach

I believe that every small business deserves expert financial guidance without the complexity and high costs typically associated with corporate accounting firms. My practice is built on clear communication, proactive planning, and a genuine commitment to your success.

Services & Expertise

With years of experience serving small businesses across various industries, I offer comprehensive tax services including:

• Quarterly estimated tax planning
• Business entity selection and optimization
• Bookkeeping and financial statement preparation
• IRS representation and audit support
• Strategic tax planning to maximize deductions
